Poetry & Lyrics

The Gray Tides

It is not the darkness that consumes you,
But the slow, endless fade to gray.
Rhythm is the only proof of pulse left,
A whisper against the deafening static of the mundane.
Where do the words go when the music stops?
They just wait.
